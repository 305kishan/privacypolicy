# Privacy Policy — Open Multiple URLs

**Last updated:** March 2026

---

## Overview

Open Multiple URLs is a Chrome browser extension that opens a list of URLs as tabs. This policy explains what data the extension handles, where it stays, and what it never does.

---

## Data We Collect

**We collect no data.**

The extension does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or store any personal information on any external server. There are no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, and no usage tracking of any kind.

---

## Data Stored Locally

The extension stores the following information exclusively in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`), on your device only:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| URL list (last entered) | Restores your input between popup sessions |
| Delay setting | Remembers your preferred tab-opening delay |
| Duplicate filter toggle | Remembers your deduplication preference |
| Preserve input toggle | Remembers your input persistence preference |
| Tab group name | Remembers your last used group name |

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to Chrome Sync, not accessible to any website, and not transmitted anywhere.

---

## Permissions Explained

### `storage`
Used solely to save your settings and last entered URL list locally on your device between popup sessions.

### `tabGroups`
Used solely to group opened tabs into a named Chrome tab group when you choose to do so via the extension UI. No tab data is read, stored, or transmitted.

---

## Third-Party Services

The extension uses no third-party services, libraries, APIs, or SDKs. It has no external network requests of any kind.

---

## Websites You Open

The extension opens URLs that you provide. It does not interact with, modify, inject into, or monitor any of the websites opened. The extension has no access to the content of those pages.

---

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not target children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including minors.

---
